About the Summit

Built by Operators,
for Operators

The Guest Experience Summit exists because the people closest to guest experience decisions had nowhere built specifically for them. We fixed that.

Our Mission

To give hospitality's most senior leaders one day a year that's entirely free of sales pitches and trade-show noise — built instead around the conversations, data and relationships that actually move guest experience forward.

Our Vision

A hospitality industry where guest experience sits permanently at the boardroom table — measured with the same rigour as revenue, and resourced accordingly.

Why This Summit Exists

A Gap We Kept Hearing About

In conversations with hotel group CEOs and COOs over several years, the same complaint surfaced again and again: existing hospitality conferences had become too broad, too vendor-heavy, and too junior in their actual attendee mix to justify a CEO's calendar.

The Guest Experience Summit was founded to solve that specific problem — a single day, exclusively C-level, with an agenda built around the one theme that every hospitality executive now agrees is existential: the guest experience itself.

We launched in London in 2024 with 90 attendees. This year, we're expecting 250 — still small enough that everyone in the room genuinely belongs there.
