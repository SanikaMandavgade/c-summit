FAQ

Common Questions

Who is eligible to attend? +
The Summit is exclusively for C-level executives, founders and senior decision-makers at hospitality groups, investment firms and technology providers serving the industry. All applications are reviewed individually.
Is there a ticket price? +
There is no public ticket sale. Attendance is by invitation following a short qualification application — see the Request Invitation page for details.
How long does the application review take? +
Most applicants hear back within five business days. We review applications on a rolling basis as capacity allows.
Can I bring a colleague? +
Each invitation is individual and non-transferable. Colleagues who also meet the eligibility criteria are welcome to apply separately.
Do you offer group rates for partners? +
Yes — partnership packages include a set number of delegate passes. See the Partners page for full package details.
