Mainstage Speaker

John Smith

CEO
Marriott International
CEO Hospitality Opening Keynote

John Smith has served as Chief Executive Officer of Marriott International since 2023, overseeing a global portfolio of more than 8,500 properties across 30 brands. Under his leadership, the company has made guest experience and loyalty technology its central strategic priority, including a company-wide initiative to unify digital and in-person service standards across every brand tier.

Before becoming CEO, John spent twelve years in senior operating roles across Marriott's luxury and premium divisions, including a tenure as President of the company's EMEA division based in London. He is a regular voice on the future of hospitality at industry forums including the World Travel & Tourism Council and the Cornell Hospitality Conference.

John holds an MBA from INSEAD and serves on the board of two hospitality technology start-ups focused on guest personalisation.
