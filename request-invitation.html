FAQ

Before You Apply

What happens after I submit? +
You'll receive an email confirmation immediately, followed by a decision within five business days. Approved applicants get a secure link to confirm their place.
What if I don't meet every criterion? +
Apply anyway. Criteria are reviewed holistically — strong influence over budget or strategy can offset a less senior title.
Is my application confidential? +
Yes. Application details are reviewed only by the Summit's delegate relations team and are never shared externally.
